Whatsup

Whatsup is a food hitching mobile app for two target groups - the people who want to order food, and the people who delivers.
Following the law of demand and supply, food orders are only fulfilled if there are people patronising the shop physically.

Whatsup started as a web application idea to combine ride-sharing and food delivery into a new concept.

Web Application

Onboarding

The onboarding screens of the hitching app orientates the user and show them the features of the app.

Login

The log in page allows the user to register as a new member or sign in as an existing member.

Landing

The user lands on a page that directs them into two different user paths.

Makan leads to the food menu and dabao leads to all the requests.

Menu

The food menu allows user to browse through the food available.

Special recommendation based on current orders is a feature to help users save money.
