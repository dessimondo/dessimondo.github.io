Roboadvisor

In this digital era, more and more users are relying on technology for their financial needs. Thus, banks have to come up with their own tools in order to bring traffic to their own products. The Roboadvisor of HSBC is a highly curated tool that helps a bank user find/customised a HSBC product to their needs.
