Why Ciconia?

Ciconia, a genus of birds in the stork family, soar on broad wings to travel the long distance. As our symbol suggests, we bring to you explorers the gift of journey - up in the clouds to the next life adventure.

Ciconia is a fully licensed travel agency and tour operator specialised in flexibly customised itineraries for individuals and private groups.
